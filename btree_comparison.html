ðŸ’¡ Why B+ trees dominate in databases:

Range queries are king! In databases, you often need to find all records between two values (e.g., "all employees with salary between $50K-$80K"). B+ trees make this lightning-fast because once you find the first value in the linked leaf chain, you just follow the links until you reach the end value. No need to go back up and down the tree!

Sequential scans: When you need to read all data in order, B+ trees let you traverse the linked leaf nodes sequentially, while B-trees require an expensive in-order tree traversal.
