ðŸŽ¯ The Bloom Filter Magic

Probabilistic Membership: Bloom filters trade a tiny chance of false positives for massive space savings and speed. No false negatives!

Hash Power: Multiple hash functions spread items across the bit array, minimizing collisions.

Real-World Impact: Used in databases, web caches, networking, and more to quickly answer "Have I seen this before?"

Scaling: Bloom filters can handle millions of items with just kilobytes of memory!
